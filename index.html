Our Heritage

Grandma's recipes.
Delivered to your door.

Koumatha Cloud Kitchen was born in a home kitchen in 2004 — founded by a passionate home chef with a single mission: bring the warmth and nourishment of true home-cooked food to every doorstep in the city.

Today, our network of experienced home chefs continues that tradition, preparing each meal fresh every morning with handpicked local vegetables, whole spices, and zero preservatives. Every dish carries the story of a grandmother's kitchen.

  • ✓ Freshly cooked every morning — never reheated or frozen
  • ✓ No artificial colours, preservatives, or MSG
  • ✓ Traditional recipes passed down 3 generations
  • ✓ Farm-direct vegetables sourced locally every day
  • ✓ Hygiene-certified kitchen with FSSAI registration
